The ideal Graphics

Crisp graphics are always superior, right? Well, while you can make that argument for games such as GTA, Fortnite, Far Cry and the like, some games pose an exception to that rule. The most popular game in the World with over 200 million sales is Minecraft, closely followed by Tetris. If your are familiar with these games, you'd know that the appeal of these titles is not the HD Graphics or the amazing ray tracing lighting effects, but the nostalgia and unique gameplay. Same goes for Pokemon.

With the first Pokemon titles being released right before the turn of the millenina and the console being a small handheld gadget, the Graphics, even for the time, were subpar. Despite that, the games managed to fetch a huge audience through the unique battle system and amazing character design which appeals to every collectors heart.

While newer switch titles fail to capture the nostalgic feel of the original Pokemon games, the Ninetndo DS was the perfect compromise of visual advancement and balance of nostalgia.

Quirks and features

Is that a Shiny Gyarados?
You're correct, Heartgold gifts you one of the coolest shiny Pokemon to ever exist. The red Gyarados has since become iconic.


Another amazing feature ahead of its time was your Pokemon following you around. Not only is it exciting to see your favourite Pokemon trail behind you, you can also interact with it and your buddy may pick up items for you.
